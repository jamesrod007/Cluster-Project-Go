The Progress Paradox

A research game about motivation, boredom, and purpose.

You are a villager who has learned that your village will soon be attacked by zombies.

The only possible chance you have of saving everyone is to find the crystals buried within a mountain that are capable of creating a protective shield around the village.

You have been selected to go on the journey to obtain the crystals.

You leave in the morning with only a small pack of food and an old pair of boots. The crystal is 100 miles away. The zombies are coming.

Both versions have the same mission and gameplay.
The difference is how progress is shown. Play the version your researcher assigned to you.
